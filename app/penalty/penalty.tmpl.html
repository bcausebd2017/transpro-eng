About translators' penalty

Penalty is imposed if there are mistakes in the translation. Although mistakes tend to occur due to translation being a human task, it is the translator's responsibility to reduce mistakes to the least before delivering it to the customer.

The penalties for translation mistakes are listed as below.

Translators with 3 penalty points will be put into a black list, and cannot receive or accept any order. Translators on the black list will not be put off the list until they improve and earn enough creditability as a translator, judge by b-cause.
